Operations Manual

Common questions, answered directly.

What is a session ID and where do I get it?
Your Instagram sessionid cookie. Extract it from your browser's developer tools under Application → Cookies. Then paste it into or directly into the sessions panel.
Why are my proxies not working?
This tool does not validate proxy format. Ensure your proxies are active and reachable. Enable "Retry with own IP" in the tab to fallback.
How does validation remove dead sessions?
Validation checks each session against Instagram. Invalid sessions are stripped from the textarea and removed from sessions_data.json automatically. Use the to review what remains.
Can I use the same session multiple times?
Duplicates are automatically deduplicated when saving. Each unique session is stored once. The shows only unique entries.

Help

Getting started and troubleshooting.

Step 1 — Load Sessions
Paste your Instagram sessionid cookies into the . One per line. Click Save to write them to sessions.txt, then switch to and click Validate to purge dead sessions automatically.
Step 2 — Configure Target
Enter the target username or paste a full Instagram link in the Target panel. Click Fetch Info to preview the account. Load your proxies in the tab if you want IP rotation.
Step 3 — Fire
Build your reporting sequence in the tab. Pick a report type, set a repeat count, and add it to the sequence. You cannot add the same report twice in a row. When ready, switch to and click Start Reporting. Live output appears in the tab.
Proxies
If you enable proxies in the tab, the tool will pick one at random for each request. If a proxy fails, the tool can fall back to your own IP if "Retry with own IP" is enabled.
My reports return "Request failed"
Instagram rate-limits aggressively. Spread sessions across longer intervals, rotate proxies, and ensure your sessions are freshly extracted from logged-in browsers.
Instagram blocks my IP
Use the tab to load a rotating proxy list. Enable "Retry with own IP" as a safety net, but your real IP may still be flagged. High-quality residential proxies are recommended.
Validation says all sessions are invalid
Sessions expire quickly. Re-extract them from an active, logged-in Instagram browser tab. Sessions from incognito or expired logins will always fail. Remove dead ones in and add fresh ones.
Can I use this without proxies?
Yes. Turn off "Use Proxies" in the tab. Your own IP will be used for every request. Best for small batches or testing.
